Soylent Software Presents: Strange

Strange is a musical toy. Maybe some day it will be a musical game.

Controls:
A/S/D/F + Mouse Movement = change shapes in different ways
Z + Mouse Movement = move a shape around
Left Mouse Button = play the tone
hold down Spacebar = sustain (use this to combine tones)
hold down Shift = reduce mouse movement speed
hold down Ctrl = reduce mouse movement speed even more
